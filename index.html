About Modeling And Animation

As mentioned earlier, I have worked in video game development. It's worth noting that when working on video games, a significant part of the work is not so much about programming, but rather about designing and animating characters, as well as the visual artistry behind it. I dare say it's almost 70% visual art and 30% code.

It's important to note that game developers can have diverse skills, including programming, graphic design, animation, sound, narrative, and more. They can work in a variety of roles, ranging from programmers and level designers to artists and writers.

While it is a competitive field due to high demand and the passion that many have for video games, the number of game developers remains substantial and continues to grow as the industry evolves.
